Our Story

About New Ebenezer MBC

"Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost." — Matthew 28:19

Founded in 1948 under the leadership of Reverend W.H. Robinson, New Ebenezer Baptist Church began in East Chicago, IN. In 1987, it became New Ebenezer Missionary Baptist Church, Inc., and in 2011 relocated to Merrillville, IN under the vision of Pastor Reverend T. Brian Hill.

Our mission continues: to baptize, educate, and evangelize while helping our community grow in faith.
