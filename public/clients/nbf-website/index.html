About Our Leader

Bishop
Kevin N. Warren

Pastor & First Lady Deadra Warren ‚Äî New Bethel Fellowship COGIC
"To God be the glory for the great things He has done."

Bishop Kevin N. Warren has served in ministry for more than 35 years, including 16 years as a pastor. Gifted in music from an early age, he has been led by the Holy Spirit to minister as a musician, composer, and minister of music.

Called to preach in 1983, Bishop Warren has led revivals where lives were transformed ‚Äî souls saved, believers filled with the Holy Ghost, and people delivered by God's power. For 15 years, he served alongside his father, Bishop E. Bobby Warren, as Co-Pastor of Deliverance Temple COGIC.

In 2002, he founded New Beginnings Fellowship COGIC and pastored there until 2014, when he was appointed Pastor of Bethel Temple COGIC. Under his leadership, the two ministries were merged and renamed New Bethel Fellowship Church of God in Christ, where he continues to faithfully shepherd the congregation in Gary, Indiana.

Beyond the church, Bishop Warren serves his community as Bishop of Supervisors for Lake County Government. He and his wife, Lady Deadra Warren, have been joyfully married for 34 years and are blessed with four children and four grandchildren.

Bishop Warren's greatest desire is to bring glory to God and fulfill His divine plan ‚Äî "To God be the glory for the great things He has done."
